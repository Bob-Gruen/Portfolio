CONNECT Analytics Machine Home

This is the Home page for customers when they scan the QR code on their machine. This is just one of the pages that I designed and built as part of Cold Jet's IoT platform, "CONNECT Analytics". This page shows insights into the customer's use of the machine, the settings they used, and the amount of ice that they used. This allowed customers to better understand their settings. In many cases, they could make adjustments that resulted in lower dry ice consumption, which equated to lower cost, and thus higher profits, without sacrificing cleaning quality.
